Why Choose Water My Plants?

We understand people are busy, and sometimes it can be easy to overlook simple tasks like watering your plants - despite the fact that those plants can play a pivotal role in the feng-shui of your home or business. We're here to help make sure your plants never go without water again, allowing you to maintain a bright & vibrant atmosphere without worry.

How It Works

Upon signing up and downloading Water My Plants, you'll have the ability to customize the watering and feeding schedule for each selected plant type. You'll be able to log every action you take for any plant you've added to your watering list. More houseplants die every year from improper watering than any other single factor; with Water My Plants, you can be assured that your rooted friends will never meet that fate again. Your plants will thank you!
